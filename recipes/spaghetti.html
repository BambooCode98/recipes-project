How to Make Spaghetti

Description

This recipe will detail how to make a simple dish of spaghetti using at most 10 ingredients!

Ingredients

  • Noodles
  • Spaghetti Sauce of your choosing
  • Ground beef, turkey, or chicken (optional)
  • Seasonings to Flavor

Steps

  1. Cook the noodles to desired tenderness
  2. Cook the chosen meat to desired temperature.
  3. Drain the pot, and put the spaghetti back into the original pot
  4. Lastly, blend the spaghetti and spaghetti sauce in the pot with the meat, and seaon to preferred flavor!
