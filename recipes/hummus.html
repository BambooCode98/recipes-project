How to Make Hummus

Description

This recipe will list all the steps need to create the perfect hummus!

ingredients

  • Chickpeas
  • Garlic
  • Tahini
  • Seasonings to Flavor>

Steps

  1. Clean the Chickpeas
  2. Blend the Chickpeas, Tahini, Garlic, and other Seasonings.
  3. When the ingredients have been pureed, put into a dish, and add garlic toppings, or any other preferred Seasonings!
